Why change Careers?

I decided to change careers as the path I was on did not align with how I foresaw my future. The physical aspect of my previous career would lead to a life of aches and pains, and the financial prospects of an archaeologist aren't much better. However, the main catalyst is work-life balance. Hard work and determination on my new path could lead to a life with much greater balance and will hopefully allow my partner and I to visit our home countries of England and Sardinia for more than one week a year.
